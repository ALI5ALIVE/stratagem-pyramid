# Three Customer-Facing Presentations — Detailed Plan

## Brief Summary
Three versions of the Comply365 Operational Performance Platform pitch for different engagement scenarios. Each weaves CoAnalyst as the overarching intelligence capability throughout the platform narrative.

---

## Presentation 1: "The Executive Pitch" (SHORT)
**Route:** `/pitch-executive`
**Audience:** C-suite, senior VPs, initial introductions, conferences
**Duration:** ~5 minutes | **Slides:** 7
**Story Arc:** Provocation → Transformation → Intelligence → Proof → Commitment

### Narrative Through-Line
*"Your operations generate millions of signals. Today, most are lost. We turn them into predictable, measurable, provable performance — powered by aviation-specific intelligence that generic AI can't deliver."*

### Slide Sequence

| # | Slide | Purpose | Content Source |
|---|-------|---------|---------------|
| 0 | **Title** | Hook & credibility | "The Operational Performance Platform" + trust bar (50+ airlines, 7/10 NA) |
| 1 | **The $47M Problem** | Provocation — the cost of fragmentation | Aggregated Line-of-Sight data: 8 costed use cases rolled up into one staggering number. Go-arounds, AOG, delays, fuel, injuries, regulatory, insurance, baggage = total annual cost of inaction |
| 2 | **The Shift** | Category-defining move | Point tools → Connected platform. Before/After in one visual. "From reactive event management to proactive control management" |
| 3 | **How It Works: DTOP** | Mechanism in 30 seconds | Detect → Trigger → Orchestrate → Prove with ONE compelling use case running through (Hard Landing → Retraining → Reduced recurrence) |
| 4 | **The Intelligence Layer** | CoAnalyst as the differentiator | CoAnalyst positioned as "Palantir for aviation operations." Hybrid AI (90% accuracy at 4,000+ categories). The Precision Gap visual: Generic AI 30-40% vs CoAnalyst 90% at granular levels. Four intelligence tiers: Historical → Reactive → Proactive → Predictive |
| 5 | **Line of Sight to Value** | CFO/CEO/COO outcomes | Three executive stakeholder cards with specific metrics. Simplified version of the Line-of-Sight cascade. Total cost avoidance number |
| 6 | **Why Comply365** | Close with conviction | 3 differentiators (Connected Foundation, Embedded Intelligence, Proof by Default) + category promise: "Predictable, measurable, provable" + clear CTA |

### Design Notes
- Highest impact-per-slide ratio. Every slide must earn its place.
- Large type, bold visuals, minimal text. Executive scanning speed.
- The $47M number is calculated from Line-of-Sight defaults — makes it tangible.
- CoAnalyst slide is the "wow moment" — the precision gap visual is the mic drop.

## Presentation 2: "The Operational Case" (MEDIUM)
**Route:** `/pitch-operational`
**Audience:** Heads of Safety, Operations, Engineering, Compliance; customers evaluating next-gen solutions
**Duration:** ~15 minutes | **Slides:** 12
**Story Arc:** Reality → Gap → Platform → Intelligence → Use Cases (Near-Term) → Stepping Stones → Vision → Outcomes → Getting Started

### Narrative Through-Line
*"You know the pain of disconnected operations. We'll show you how a connected platform — with aviation-specific intelligence at its core — transforms your daily reality, starting with use cases you can achieve this year."*

### Slide Sequence

| # | Slide | Purpose | Content Source |
|---|-------|---------|---------------|
| 0 | **Title** | Set the stage | "From Signals to Outcomes" + audience-specific framing (Safety, Ops, or Training) |
| 1 | **Your Daily Reality** | Empathy — show we understand | "Day in the Life" timeline from SalesSlide1. Pain points grid. Emotional resonance |
| 2 | **The Cost of Fragmentation** | Quantify the problem | Metrics with animation (12K signals, 8K orphaned, 3-week investigations). Risk compounding timeline |
| 3 | **Before & After** | The transformation visual | Connected vs fragmented data visualization. DTOP summary. Illustrative outcomes |
| 4 | **The Platform: DTOP in Action** | How it works | Full DTOP pipeline with ecosystem diagram. Data sources → pipeline → value outputs. Interactive step exploration |
| 5 | **CoAnalyst: The Intelligence Layer** | CoAnalyst deep-dive | Positioned as the intelligence engine powering DTOP. 5-step pipeline: Ingest → Enrich → Detect → Intelligence → Activate. Hybrid AI architecture, 90% accuracy, 4,000+ categories. Four intelligence tiers with real examples |
| 6 | **Near-Term Use Cases** | Achievable this year | 3-4 specific use cases that demonstrate immediate value: Hard landing retraining, Smoke & fumes procedure change, Regulatory change cascade, Crew fatigue pattern detection. Each shows Signal → Platform Action → Outcome |
| 7 | **Stepping Stones** | From near-term to platform capability | Visual journey: Quick Wins (Year 1) → Connected Operations (Year 2) → Intelligent Operations (Year 3). Shows how near-term use cases build toward full platform value |
| 8 | **The Maturity Roadmap** | Where you're headed | 5-stage maturity curve: Fragmented → Managed → Connected → Intelligent → Predictive. "You are here" positioning. Each stage with a concrete use case |
| 9 | **Operational Outcomes** | Tangible results | 4 outcome boxes (Schedule Protection, Revenue Protection, Cost Savings, Customer Loyalty). Signal → Action → Result framework. Time allocation shift (60% coordination → 70% improvement) |
| 10 | **Why Comply365** | Competitive positioning | Connected Foundation, Embedded Intelligence (CoAnalyst), Proof by Default. Trust bar. Category promise |
| 11 | **Getting Started** | Clear next step | Discovery workshop offer. "What does your first use case look like?" Engagement model |

### Design Notes
- More depth per slide than Executive, but still visual-first.
- Near-term use cases are critical — this audience needs to see "what can I do THIS year."
- Stepping stones slide bridges tactical (near-term) to strategic (platform vision).
- CoAnalyst is woven as the intelligence engine that powers everything, not a separate product.

### Design Notes
- This deck is encyclopedic — it's the "leave-behind" for evaluation teams.
- Technical audiences want architecture diagrams, data flow, and costed models.
- Three dedicated use case slides (Safety, Ops, Financial) give depth per domain.
- CoAnalyst gets TWO dedicated slides (architecture + intelligence tiers) plus the vs Generic AI comparison.
- The 2026 Roadmap slide is placeholder content — flagged for customer to provide specifics.
- Line-of-Sight calculator is simplified (static) but references the full interactive tool.

## Implementation Notes

### Shared Components
All three decks will use:
- `SalesSlideContainer` for consistent branding (Comply365 logo, slide numbers)
- `SlideNarrationProps` interface for future narration support
- Existing data from `lineOfSightData.ts` for costed use cases
- Shared design tokens from the existing design system

### New Components Needed
- `ExecSlide*` components (7 slides) in `src/components/exec-slides/`
- `OpsSlide*` components (12 slides) in `src/components/ops-slides/`
- `TechSlide*` components (18 slides) in `src/components/tech-slides/`
- 3 new page components in `src/pages/`
- Sidebar navigation entries for all 3 decks

### CoAnalyst Integration Pattern
CoAnalyst is NOT presented as a separate product in any deck. Instead:
- **Executive:** "The Intelligence Layer" — one powerful slide showing the precision gap
- **Operational:** "The Intelligence Engine" — how CoAnalyst powers DTOP with near-term examples
- **Technical:** "Aviation Intelligence Platform" — full architecture, tiers, and vs Generic AI

### Content Reuse Strategy
- Line-of-Sight cost data → Executive "$47M Problem" slide
- DTOP operating model → All three decks (increasing detail)
- Before/After visualization → Executive (simplified) + Operational + Technical
- Maturity Curve → Operational + Technical
- Use case data → All three (1 example in Exec, 4 in Ops, 8 in Technical)
- CoAnalyst precision gap → All three (visual in Exec, detail in Ops/Tech)
